Triple Canopy: Design, creative direction, editorial design, and more (2007-2022)

This collection is a small sampling of work spanning my 15 years as creative and technology director of Triple Canopy. What is included are different iterations of the Triple Canopy website, examples from all of the content presentation frameworks—ALS, Horizonize, Alongslide, and b-ber—some digital and print publications and a few tote bags for good measure.

There were many software engineers and designers who contributed to this work, but since it is difficult to neatly parse who did what—the first image included involves the work of four designers, as well as multiple software engineers (the publications and events were produced with different frameworks)—I’ll list below the different iterations of the website and who worked on them with me. Additionally, for issues 19-27, TC had custom identities for each issue, sometimes done in-house, sometimes as a commission, which are outlined as well.
